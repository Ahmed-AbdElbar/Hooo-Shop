Poodle dog


Breed Overview

GROUP: Non-Sporting

HEIGHT: Up to 10 inches (toy), 10 to 15 inches (miniature), over 15 inches (standard)

LIFE SPAN: 10 to 18 years

TEMPERAMENT: Intelligent, affectionate, active

HYPOALLERGENIC: Yes

ORIGIN: Germany
